Formula Components

Design Score = (Impact × 1.5) × (Expectations × 1.2) × Timeliness Modifier × Bonuses

Timeliness Modifier = Max(0, 1 - (Days Late × Impact × 0.03))

On-Time Bonus = 1.1 if Days Late = 0, otherwise 1.0

Expectations Bonus = 1.1 if Expectations ≥ 2, otherwise 1.0
